This page illustrates the clickthrough pattern. This is not a formal concept in IIIF Auth 2.0 (unlike Auth 1.0).

Clicking the button below is, in this example, the user's interaction with the content provider. In this demo we establish a cookie that the token service can see. In practice, anything could be happening in this window.

This might be some text describing terms of use, or a notice about the sensitivity of the content, or anything.

Clicking either of these buttons will seem to close the window, but with different outcomes.
